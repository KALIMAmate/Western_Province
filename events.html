Events in Western Province

Experience the vibrant culture and rich heritage of Western Province through its diverse events and festivals.

Upcoming Events and Festivals

Join in the celebration of Western Province's unique cultural identity.

  • Kazungula Cultural Festival: A showcase of traditional music, dance, and art.
  • Livingstone International Cultural Festival: A fusion of local and international cultures.
  • Western Province Agricultural Show: Promoting agricultural innovation and excellence.

Cultural Events and Traditional Ceremonies

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Western Province.

  • Lozi Traditional Ceremony: A celebration of Lozi culture and tradition.
  • Nkoya Traditional Ceremony: A showcase of Nkoya customs and rituals.
  • Kaonde Traditional Ceremony: A celebration of Kaonde heritage.

Outdoor Activities

Explore the natural beauty of Western Province through various outdoor activities.

  • Hiking at Victoria Falls: Experience the majestic wonder of the falls.
  • Fishing at Lake Kariba: Enjoy world-class fishing in stunning surroundings.
  • Game drives in Liuwa Plains National Park: Witness the incredible wildlife.
